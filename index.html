About Me

My name is Szymon Gustav Snoeck and I am a fourth-year applied mathematics major at Columbia University minoring in computer science. My main research interests are in machine learning and high dimensional statistics. However through guided research projects and graduate classes, I have also done research in algorithmic analysis, nearest neighbor search, learning theory, matching theory, dimension-reduction, and derandomization. Some of the work I have produced can be found below.
